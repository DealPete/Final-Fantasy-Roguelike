@Use the arrow keys to move about the map.

>   Go down stairs, enter a town, or explore an area.
<   Go up stairs.

c   Cast a spell.                    e   Equip item.
J   Allocate job points.             i   Use item.
m   Browse the map.                  I   Use key item.
s   View the status screen.          r   Switch rows.

P   Browse the message buffer.       S   Save and quit.
Q   Quit without saving.

SPACE, z, Z, ENTER    Select/Continue.
ESC, x, X             Cancel/Close window.
